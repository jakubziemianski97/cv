Bouncy animals

The project is based on a mobile game popular a few years ago. The main goal of the player is to score as many points as possible by jumping between "pipes". To make the game more interesting, different heroes and themes have been added, and the space between "pipes" decreases every 2 points scored. The game, among other things, uses functions that generate "pipes" at a random height, check for collisions with "pipes," and adds non-linear hero falling to make it look natural.
